Artemis Launch

On November 16, 2022, Artemis 1 was launched, project that aims to return man to the moon. Since the end of the cold war, space exploration has had low incentives and low investments, and this leads to a technological delay for all of humanity. But this time, the United States, through NASA, decided to create the Artemis missions, to resume the era of space exploration, and bring a technological advance to the world.

This mission was not manned, but to transport probes to the lunar surface as there is on Mars. This whole process is aimed at a possible human colony on the moon
